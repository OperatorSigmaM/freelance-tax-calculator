California State Income Tax Guide 2025

California has one of the highest state income tax rates in the United States, with a progressive tax system featuring 9 tax brackets ranging from 1% to 12.3%, plus an additional 1% Mental Health Services Tax on income over $1 million.

Key Features of California Taxes

Progressive Tax System: California uses a graduated tax system where higher income is taxed at higher rates.

Mental Health Services Tax: Income over $1 million is subject to an additional 1% tax, bringing the maximum marginal rate to 13.3% for very high earners.

No Local Income Tax: California does not allow cities or counties to impose additional local income taxes.

Common California Tax Deductions

  • Standard Deduction: $5,540 for single filers
  • Dependent Exemption: $446 per dependent
  • Business Expenses: Same as federal
  • Retirement Contributions: 401(k), IRA
