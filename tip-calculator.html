Tipping Guide and Calculator

Tipping etiquette can be confusing, especially when dining with groups or traveling to different countries. Our tip calculator makes it easy to calculate fair gratuity and split bills among friends.

Standard Tipping Guidelines

  • 15%: Standard service at restaurants
  • 18-20%: Good service or large groups
  • 20-25%: Excellent service
  • 10%: Acceptable for poor service (though you may want to speak with management)

When to Tip More

Consider tipping on the higher end (20-25%) when:

  • Dining at expensive restaurants
  • Service was exceptional
  • Server went above and beyond
  • You had a complicated order or dietary restrictions

Splitting Bills

When dining with friends, use the "Number of People" field to calculate each person's share. The calculator automatically divides the total (including tip) evenly among all diners.

Tip: Calculate the tip based on the pre-tax amount for the most accurate gratuity.
