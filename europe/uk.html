Dividend Tax Rates 2025/26

Dividend income is taxed differently from employment or self-employment income:

  • Dividend Allowance: First ¬£500 tax-free
  • Basic Rate: 8.75%
  • Higher Rate: 33.75%
  • Additional Rate: 39.35%

Important Note: Scotland

Scotland has different income tax rates and bands from the rest of the UK (England, Wales, and Northern Ireland). This calculator uses the UK-wide rates and does not include the Scottish tax rates.
